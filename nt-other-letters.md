## Books in this section

* [Hebrews](hebrews.md): shows how Jesus is described as fulfilling and surpassing earlier worship[^gl_worship] patterns.
* [James](james.md): practical teaching about wise speech, fairness, and actions matching beliefs.
* [1 Peter](1-peter.md): encouragement for Christians facing pressure and misunderstanding.
* [2 Peter](2-peter.md): warnings about false teaching and reminders about patience and hope.
* [1 John](1-john.md): love, truth, and testing what teachings lead to.
* [2 John](2-john.md): a short warning about teachers who mislead communities.
* [3 John](3-john.md): a short letter about hospitality and leadership behaviour.
* [Jude](jude.md): a short, strong warning against harmful influences.
